Cinnamon Roasted Pineapple

Description

A favourite at parties, serve this dish warm and you are sure to be invited back. Taken from Jessica Seinfeld's cookbook: VEGAN, at Times.

Ingredients

  • 1 pineapple
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp flaky sea salt
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Preparation

  1. Heat the oven to 450F
  2. Cut off the top and bottom of the pineapple, then trim off the skin.
  3. Cut the pineapple into quarters lengthwise and cut the long core from each piece.
  4. Slice the pineapple crosswise into 1/2 inch pieces.
  5. Put the pineapple pieces on a baking tray lined with parchment paper in a single layer.
  6. Sprinkle with the cinnamon.
  7. Roast, stirring halfway through, until tender and carmelized, 35 to 45 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle with the salt and serve with lime wedges.
