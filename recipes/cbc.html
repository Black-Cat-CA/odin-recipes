Chicken Broccoli and Cheese

Description

This cozy casserole is a family favourite on cold winter evenings. It can be made in ~40 minutes so it is a great weekday meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 10-oz pkg frozen (or fresh) broccoli
  • 1 10-oz can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 10-oz can cream of celery soup
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 2-4 cups cooked rice

Preparation

  1. Cut chicken into 1/2 in cubes and sprinkle with pepper
  2. Saute slowly in oil over medium heat until white and opaque, about 6 minutes; drain
  3. Arrange broccoli in bottom of casserole dish; cover with cooked chicken
  4. Mix soup, mayonnaise, curry powder and lemon juice; pour over chicken
  5. Sprinkle top with cheddar cheese
  6. Bake uncovered at 375 degrees for 30-35 minutes
  7. Serve over cooked rice
