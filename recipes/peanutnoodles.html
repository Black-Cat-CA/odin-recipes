Cold Peanut Noodles

Description

Great for mealprep. These noodles only take 10 minutes to make and pack a ton of flavour and can be eaten warm or cold. Taken from Jessica Seinfeld's cookbook: VEGAN, at Times.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti noodles (I substitue chickpea pasta)
  • 1/4 cup tamari or soy sauce
  • 1/4 peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp Sriracha
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp grated, peeled fresh ginger (or sub 1 tsp ground ginger)
  • pinch of salt, to taste
  • 1 cucumber
  • 3 scallions, thinly sliced
  • 1/3 cup chopped roasted, salted peanuts

Preparation

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package directions then drain and rinse under cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the tamari, peanut butter, vinegar, sesame oil, Sriracha, brown sugar, ginger, and salt.
  3. Toss the noodles thoroughly in the sauce mix.
  4. Quarter the cucumber lengthwise, then slice it crosswise into small pieces.
  5. Divide the noodles among bowls and top with cucumber, scallions, peanuts, and extra Sriracha if you want more heat.
